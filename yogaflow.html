Yoga Flow

The Challenge

Yoga Flow is an app built for iOS and Android that allows users to learn and customize a yoga practice.

User Goals

  • WHO: Anyone interested in learning yoga, and who wants to better their physical & mental wellbeing. Generally geared towards mid-level beginners who understand the basics and have a desire to learn more.
  • WHAT: Users will be able to view yoga poses and sequence them in the order that they’d want to work through the poses.
  • WHEN: My audience will use this app when they want to get a yoga workout in and when they want to stretch and work on different parts of the body, i.e. lower back, arms, etc.
  • WHERE: At home, or at the gym.
  • WHY: My product is more suited to be a mobile app because there will be the ability to have their yoga practice on any device e.g. mobile, tablet for ease of use, as well as the ability to cast the workouts to a TV or other device.

Design Requirements

  • Must follow iOS and Material Design guidelines
  • iOS and Android native mobile patterns used
  • User gestures, sounds, and haptic feedback annotated
  • Appropriate use of typography and color
  • UI must be intuitive and consistent throughout

Key Features

  • Learning new yoga poses
  • Sequence yoga poses together to create a flow
  • Watch tutorial videos on flows

Retrospective

In building Yoga Flow, I did not encounter too many challenges. Many of my initial ideas were incorporated into the final app, and I’m very happy with the outcome. If I had to name a challenge, I would say that deciding on the visualization of a yoga pose was a difficult step, as it’s hard to find consistency between images for yoga poses. I did some research, and found these illustrations that worked really well with my end goal.

I plan to improve on the video portion of this app in a future iteration, as this is one of my key features that is difficult to visualize in a flat design. I’d like to animate the play button to disappear once the video for the yoga pose and/or flow starts, and show that it will change from a play button to a pause button, in accordance with what the user is engaged with at the moment.
